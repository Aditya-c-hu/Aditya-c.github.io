Environmental Impact of Port Activities

Movement of Goods and Cargo

The movement of goods and cargo involves the transportation of products and materials from one location to another. This process is critical for global trade and the supply chain. It includes various modes of transport such as ships, trucks, trains, and airplanes. Efficient cargo movement reduces costs and delivery times, ensuring that products reach consumers and businesses promptly.

Sorting of Goods in Port

Sorting of goods in port involves organizing and categorizing cargo based on various factors such as destination, content, and priority. This process ensures efficient handling, storage, and transfer, reducing delays and optimizing space utilization. Proper sorting is critical for maintaining the flow of goods and minimizing handling times.
