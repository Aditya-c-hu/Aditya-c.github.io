Environmental Impact of Port Activities

Key Issues

Marine Pollution: Oil spills, ballast water discharge, and ship waste can pollute coastal waters.

Air Emissions: Port operations, including ship docking, cargo handling, and transportation, contribute to air pollution (CO2, NOx, and SOx emissions).

Solid Waste: Cargo handling generates packaging waste, while maintenance activities produce hazardous waste (e.g., paint, solvents).

Dredging and Sediment Waste: Dredging for port expansion disrupts marine habitats and generates large volumes of sediment waste.
