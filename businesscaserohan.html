Environmental Impact of the Transportation Sector

Key Issues

Air Pollution: Fossil fuel-powered vehicles release CO2, NOx, and particulate matter, contributing to smog and respiratory diseases.

Waste Generation: End-of-life vehicles (ELVs), tires, batteries, and packaging from logistics operations create significant waste.

Noise Pollution: Excessive noise from transportation affects urban living and wildlife.

Traffic Congestion: Leads to higher emissions and fuel wastage, increasing pollution levels.
