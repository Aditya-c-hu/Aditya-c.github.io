City Design Overview

We were given the task of designing a smart city spanning 1500 sq km, with a focus on being realistic, practical, and future-ready.

Our journey began with a thorough analysis of the geography, weather conditions, and geological aspects of the area. This helped us create a city layout that aligns with natural resources and environmental conditions.

Anantavarm is built on the pillars of sustainability, innovation, and growth. Every aspect, from infrastructure to resource management, was designed thoughtfully to meet these goals.

Anantavarm

Anantavaram is a visionary smart city near the city of Surat, designed for sustainability, efficiency, and innovation. The city integrates technology and natural resources to create a thriving urban environment.
