Environmental Impact of Water Pipeline Systems

Key Issues

Construction Waste: Excavation, pipeline installation, and material usage produce concrete, plastic, and metal waste.

Water Leakage and Contamination: Aging pipelines or poor maintenance can lead to water leaks, contaminating soil and wasting resources.

Chemical Pollution: Use of chemicals for pipeline maintenance (e.g., cleaning agents and sealants) can lead to water pollution if not handled properly.

Excavation and Soil Displacement: Digging trenches for pipelines disturbs ecosystems and generates soil waste.
